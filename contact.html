Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start care services?

We can typically begin care services within 24-48 hours of initial consultation, and sometimes same-day for urgent needs.

Do you accept insurance or Medicare?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans, Medicare, Medicaid, and offer private pay options. Contact us to verify your coverage.

Are your caregivers background checked?

Absolutely. All our caregivers undergo comprehensive background checks, reference verification, and skills assessments.

Can I request a specific caregiver?

Yes! We work to match you with a compatible caregiver and honor requests for specific caregivers when available.

What if I need to change my care schedule?

We offer flexible scheduling. Contact your care coordinator to adjust your schedule as needs change.

Do you provide staffing 24/7?

Yes, our staffing department operates 24/7 to meet healthcare facilities' urgent and emergency staffing needs.
